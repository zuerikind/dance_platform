# Roadmap: Bailadmin

## Overview

This roadmap delivers trial stability, growth beyond the two trial schools, and incremental admin and student features. Phases are derived from v1 requirements: Trial (4), Growth (2), Admin (1), Student (1). Every v1 requirement maps to exactly one phase.

## Phases

### Phase 1: Trial stability & support

**Goal:** Trial runs smoothly; schools and students are satisfied; no critical issues; schools can convert to month-by-month payment.

**Dependencies:** None (foundation for growth).

**Requirements:** TRIAL-01, TRIAL-02, TRIAL-03, TRIAL-04

**Success criteria:**

1. Critical bugs reported during trial are fixed; previously broken flows work as expected.
2. Small UX, clarity, or performance improvements are in place where needed; trial experience is improved.
3. Schools have documentation for using admin, schedules, students, QR, and packages; they can self-serve.
4. No data loss; attendance and payment flows are reliable; schools and students report satisfaction and trial runs without critical bugs.

---

### Phase 2: Growth

**Goal:** Platform can onboard and support more schools beyond the two trial schools; operations and performance scale.

**Dependencies:** Phase 1 (trial success enables growth).

**Requirements:** GROW-01, GROW-02

**Success criteria:**

1. New schools beyond the trial two can be onboarded via a defined flow or process.
2. Platform performs and operates reliably for more schools and students; support and operations are in place to scale.

---

### Phase 3: Admin features

**Goal:** Admins have new or improved features as identified during trial or growth (scoped for this phase).

**Dependencies:** Phase 1 (trial feedback); Phase 2 recommended for broader input.

**Requirements:** ADMIN-01

**Success criteria:**

1. Admin improvements or new features identified for this phase are delivered and usable by schools.

---

### Phase 4: Student features

**Goal:** Students have new or improved features as identified (scoped for this phase).

**Dependencies:** Phase 1 (trial feedback); Phase 2 recommended for scale context.

**Requirements:** STUDENT-01

**Success criteria:**

1. Student-facing improvements or new features identified for this phase are delivered and usable.
