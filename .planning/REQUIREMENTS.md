# Requirements: Bailadmin

**Defined:** 2025-03-02
**Core Value:** Dance schools have a tool to manage students and revenue; students have all their schools and packages in one place.

## v1 Requirements

Requirements for trial success, stability, growth, and incremental new features. Each maps to roadmap phases.

### Trial

- [ ] **TRIAL-01**: Critical bugs and issues reported during trial are fixed
- [ ] **TRIAL-02**: Small improvements that improve trial experience (UX, clarity, performance where needed)
- [ ] **TRIAL-03**: Documentation for schools (how to use admin, schedules, students, QR, packages)
- [ ] **TRIAL-04**: Trial runs smoothly: no data loss, reliable attendance and payment flows, schools and students satisfied

### Growth

- [ ] **GROW-01**: Onboard new schools beyond the two trial schools (onboarding flow or process)
- [ ] **GROW-02**: Platform scales for more schools and students (performance, support, operations)

### Features (Admin)

- [ ] **ADMIN-01**: New or improved admin features as identified during trial or growth (scoped per phase)

### Features (Student)

- [ ] **STUDENT-01**: New or improved student-facing features as identified (scoped per phase)
