# Bailadmin – Guide for schools

Bailadmin helps dance schools manage students, classes, attendance, and payments in one place. This guide is for school staff and admins who use Bailadmin day to day.

Use the sections below to find step-by-step instructions for common tasks.

---

## Admin

School settings, managing admins, and platform options.

→ **[Admin](schools/admin.md)** – How to configure your school and who can manage it.

---

## Schedules

Classes, calendar, and private lessons. Set up your weekly schedule and optional Calendly booking for private teachers.

→ **[Schedules](schools/schedules.md)** – Classes, calendar, and private lesson booking.

---

## Students

Adding and editing students, linking them to accounts, and managing their packages.

→ **[Students](schools/students.md)** – Add students, edit details, and link packages.

---

## QR and attendance

Check in students and record attendance using the QR scanner. One class is deducted from the student’s package when you confirm.

→ **[QR and attendance](schools/qr.md)** – Use the QR scanner to take attendance.

---

## Packages and payments

Create packages (e.g. 10 classes), send payment requests to students, and mark payments as received.

→ **[Packages and payments](schools/packages.md)** – Packages, payment requests, and marking paid.

---

*For technical setup (e.g. Calendly integration), see the other guides in the `docs/` folder.*
