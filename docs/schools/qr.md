# QR scanner and attendance

School staff use the QR scanner to check in students and record attendance. When you confirm, one class is deducted from the student’s package.

## How to take attendance

1. Open the **QR** view (QR icon in the navigation).
2. Allow camera access when the app asks.
3. The student shows their QR code (from their student card or the app).
4. After the scan, choose **Confirm attendance** (or confirm for the class shown if the student is pre-registered).
5. You’ll see **“Attendance confirmed!”** when it’s done. One class is then deducted from the student’s package.

If the scan doesn’t work, check that the camera has permission and that the QR code is clearly visible. You can also look up the student in the app and record attendance from their profile if your school supports it.

## Tips

- Use the QR view in a well-lit area so the code scans quickly.
- If a student is already registered for that class, the app may show the class name before you confirm—check and then confirm.
- After “Attendance confirmed!”, the student’s remaining classes are updated automatically.
