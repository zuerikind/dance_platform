# Students – Adding, editing, and packages

School staff use Bailadmin to add students, update their details, and link them to packages. This page explains how.

## Adding a student

1. Open the **Students** area.
2. Click **Add student** (or similar).
3. Enter the student’s name and any required details (e.g. email, phone).
4. Save. The student appears in your student list.

You can assign packages to the student after they’re added (see Packages below).

## Editing a student

1. Go to **Students** and open the student (click their name or row).
2. Change the details you need (name, email, phone, etc.).
3. Save your changes.

Edits are saved immediately for that student.

## Linking to an account

If a student has (or creates) a Bailadmin account, you can link their profile to that account so they can see their packages and book classes.

1. Open the student in the **Students** area.
2. Use the option to **Link account** or **Connect account** (wording may vary).
3. Follow the steps—for example, the student signs in or you enter the linked account—and confirm.

Once linked, the student can log in and see their packages and bookings for your school.

## Packages

To give a student a package (e.g. 10 classes):

1. Open the student.
2. Go to the **Packages** or **Subscriptions** section for that student.
3. Use **Add package** (or assign an existing package), choose the package type and number of classes if applicable, and save.

After that, you can take attendance (e.g. via the QR scanner) and the system will deduct one class from their package when you confirm. For creating package types and payment requests, see **[Packages and payments](packages.md)**.
