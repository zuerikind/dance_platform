# Admin – School settings and admins

As a school admin you can change your school’s details and manage who has admin access. This page explains how.

## School settings

1. Open **Settings** (or the settings / gear icon in the app).
2. Update your school name, contact details, or other options shown there.
3. Save your changes when you’re done.

Your school’s public profile and how students see you are based on these settings.

## Managing admins

1. Go to **Settings** (or the admin area for your school).
2. Find the section for **Admins** or **Team**.
3. To add an admin: use the option to invite or add a user; enter their email or account details as prompted.
4. To remove an admin: open the admins list and use the remove or revoke option next to that person.

Only current admins can add or remove other admins. Make sure at least one admin remains for the school.

## Platform settings

If your school uses optional features (e.g. private teacher booking, integrations), those options usually appear under Settings. Turn them on or off as needed and save. For technical setup (e.g. connecting Calendly), see the setup guides in the docs folder.
