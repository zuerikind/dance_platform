# Schedules – Classes, calendar, and private lessons

You use Bailadmin to manage your weekly classes, see the calendar, and—for private teachers—offer bookable private lessons. This page explains how.

## Classes and weekly schedule

1. Open the **Schedules** or **Classes** area in the app.
2. To add a class: use **Add class** (or similar). Enter the class name, day, time, and any other details asked for.
3. To change a class: open it from the list or calendar and edit the details, then save.
4. To remove a class: open it and use the delete or remove option. Confirm if prompted.

Your classes appear on the calendar and students can sign up according to your school’s setup.

## Calendar

1. Go to the **Calendar** view (or Schedules if the calendar is there).
2. Use the calendar to see classes by day or week.
3. Click a class to see details or to manage sign-ups and attendance.

The calendar shows the same classes you defined in your schedule.

## Private lessons (and Calendly)

If your school is set up as a **private teacher**, you can offer bookable private lessons. Students see your availability and book a slot; the booking is recorded in Bailadmin and a private credit is deducted.

1. In **Settings**, look for the **Calendly** or **Private booking** section.
2. Connect your Calendly account and choose the event type used for “Private class booking” (follow the steps in the app).
3. Once connected, students can book via the booking view; you’ll see the bookings and deducted credits in Bailadmin.

For full Calendly setup (OAuth, webhooks, secrets), see **[Calendly integration for private teachers](../CALENDLY_SETUP.md)** in the docs.
